The Quantum Superposition Concept

The logo represents quantum superposition where |0⟩ + |1⟩ = Q

  • 0 (the circle) = Traditional banking foundation
  • 1 (the angled line) = Digital future and innovation
  • Q (the fusion) = Quantum Bank bridging both worlds
  • Quantum particles = Uncertainty, probability, cutting-edge tech
  • 8° tilt = Intentional flow creating perfect Q-tail symmetry

The subtle tilt of the "1" makes it flow naturally as the tail of the Q, while maintaining its identity as a binary digit. Both serifs are perfectly symmetrical, creating a balanced, professional mark that works at any size.
